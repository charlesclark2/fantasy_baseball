=== OVERVIEW ===

Closing Line Value (CLV) is computed as:
  clv_home_ml = close_vf_home - open_vf_home
  clv_total   = close_total_line - open_total_line

  Opening snapshot: first snapshot on game day (ET date = game_date).
  Closing snapshot: last snapshot strictly before commence_time (game start).
  Vig-free method: additive (live 2026+ data only).
    vf_home = (1/home_decimal) / (1/home_decimal + 1/away_decimal)
  Historical (2021-2025): home_win_prob from odds_snapshots_historical used
  as vig-free approximation (accurate to within ~2-3%).

  Positive mean_clv_ml means the market moved toward the home team winning
  on average, confirming the model's predictions were ahead of the consensus.

=== INTERPRETATION ===

1. Mean CLV (+0.0027 overall) is consistently positive across 2021-2025.
   This is a good signal that historical predictions were, on average, made
   before the market moved toward home teams. It does NOT yet indicate edge
   specific to OUR model's picks (that analysis requires directional CLV).

2. Model version trend (v0 → v1 → v2): mean CLV improves with each version.
   v2 shows the highest CLV across all metrics. This is preliminary evidence
   that newer model versions are better at identifying pre-market consensus.

3. Totals CLV (-0.0285) is slightly negative: the market moves down (toward
   under) by close on average. This aligns with the known phenomenon that
   sharp money in totals markets typically pushes lines down.

4. Low pct_positive_clv for 2025-Aug/Sep and 2026-Apr: investigate whether
   these periods have data quality issues or reflect genuine market dynamics.
