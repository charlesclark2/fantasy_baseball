## 0. The two value cases (why NCAAF is in scope вҖ” read first)
1. **NCAAF's own betting/analytics product вҖ” a STRETCH** on the fall timeline (huge talent disparity, scheme/pace variance, but *soft markets on smaller programs* = where a real edge is most plausible). Phase it; don't over-invest before it earns.

## 2. рҹҺҜ DATA вҖ” exactly what we're looking for (the crux; drives Phase 0)
**Minimum bar = full box scores (team + per-player game logs). Reach = position-level ADVANCED metrics.** The honest availability map (grounds the Phase-0 source decision):
