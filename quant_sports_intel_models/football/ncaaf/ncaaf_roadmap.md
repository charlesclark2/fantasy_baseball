## 0. The two value cases (why NCAAF is in scope вЂ” read first)
1. **NCAAF's own betting/analytics product вЂ” a STRETCH** on the fall timeline (huge talent disparity, scheme/pace variance, but *soft markets on smaller programs* = where a real edge is most plausible). Phase it; don't over-invest before it earns.

## 4. в­ђ Why NCAAF is where the closed-MLB tools get their real test
