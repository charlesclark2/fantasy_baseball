## ⚠️ Leakage caveat — the mean-CV headline is CONTAMINATED; 2026 is the only honest read

The Layer 3 features are sub-model outputs (run_env_v4 / offense_v2 / bullpen / starter) generated by `generate_*_signals.py`, which loads the single final sub-model artifact and predicts **all** backfilled 2021+ games (no walk-forward / as-of-date). Per `sub_model_registry.yaml` those sub-models are trained on **2021–2025**, so for 2023–2025 the features are **in-sample sub-model predictions** — leakage that Layer-3 walk-forward CV cannot catch. 2026 is excluded from sub-model training, so it is the **only leakage-free eval season**.

Effect (A2 +Platt per-season Brier): 2023 **0.1840**, 2024 **0.1850**, 2025 **0.1864** — *below any plausible market Brier* (impossible for a market-blind model) — then 2026 **0.2220** (ECE 0.074, 4× worse). The mean-CV Brier **0.1943 is invalid for promotion**.

**Honest 2026 model-vs-market (de-vigged Bovada, same-season covered games):**
| season | market Brier | model Brier |
|---|---|---|
| 2026 (clean OOS) | **0.1967** (n=807) | **0.2220** (n=668) |

On the only clean season **the market beats the model by ~0.025 Brier** — Approach B shows **no edge**. This mirrors the Epic 10 totals verdict (see `totals_bayesian_evaluation.md`): on leakage-free data the market-blind Layer 3 model does not beat Bovada. **Gates 11.4 / 11.7.** The numbers below are retained for the record but must be read through this caveat.

## Beta representation for Approach B (uncertainty)

The classifier emits a point `p_home_win`; its global ECE (0.0171) is a *coarse, dataset-level* calibration measure, not a per-game uncertainty. Using ECE as a single Beta concentration would assign every game the same uncertainty — wrong for the Epic 19 gate, which needs `win_prob_uncertainty` to widen on low-signal / early-season games. The principled per-game uncertainty is Approach A's `win_prob_to_beta` (concentration from the joint run-distribution `combined_sigma`, Epic 9 stacking). **Recommendation:** if Approach B wins 11.4, pair its `p_home_win` with the Epic 9 `combined_sigma` to form the Beta, rather than an ECE-derived constant. Documented here per the 11.3 task.
